STEPS FOR CREATING SNAPSHOT AND INCREASING VOLUME OF ROOT DRIVE
CREATE A WINDOWS INSTANCE
1.click on the volumes change the name to webserver 
2.right click on volumes and click create snapshot name it as webserverwith50gb
give the extension
3.click on the snapshot id
4.right click on webserverwith50gb and click create volume
5.give the range to extend as 50gb and make the availability zone same as the availability zone of the instance created
6.create volume > click on volume
7.give the name webwith50gb
8.right click on webserver and click detach 
9.click on the reload button on the right top
10.click on the webwith50gb
11.In the popup dialog box paste the 
-instance with windows instance id 
-device with root volume of webserver
12.Goto instance select launch instance
13.Right click on localdiskc >disk management
14.click on extend volume click next >finish
